+Restricted and Privileged Access to Techniques

1) A Title cannot give you access above your rank to a technique group. If a Title gives you b Rank 1 - 3 Kata, you get access to Rank 1 Kata at Rank 1, Rank 1-2 Kata at Rank 2 etc.
RAW: Yoriki Title gives you rank 1-3 Kata access, even if you are Rank 1, even if you don't 'naturally' get Kata access.
Rationale: Abusable.

2) If your curriculum or title gives you access to a technique or technique group, you retain access even when you rank up in your curriculum or when you complete your title.
RAW: As per FAQ, if you rank up or if you complete the title, you lose access to special access (i.e. if you are Shosuro Infiltrator, if you don't buy Skulk during Rank 1, you can't buy it at Rank 2 or above)
Rationale: I don't see why they would forbid it to be taught to you. I don't see what this adds or enhances.

+Duel House Rules

1) When striking or otherwise making an attack, if the strike misses, the attacker takes the defender's vigilance in strife.
RAW: There is no penalty to missing a strike in a duel.
Rationale: This is to stop duels becoming two people slashing wildly at the air around each other until one connects - pretty sure duels should not look like this - as with the rules as written, this is pretty much the most effective tactic. When there is a penalty to missing, Center action becomes useful. A high composure character can still slash wildly all around a low vigilance opponent for a while before he strifes out, but it is no longer the best strategy for everyone.

+Mirumoto Niten User Nerf

1) Mirumoto Niten school ability can only be used with swords. No using karate chops with it, no using it with kusari-gama, no using it with anything other than swords.
RAW: You can use it with your hands, or with anything you want.
Rationale: Abusable, unthematic, OP.

+Prone House Rule

1) Prone characters get +2 to all their TNs.
RAW: Prone is an advantage, try not to make your opponents prone because it will make them stronger, it doesn't make anything more difficult for them except they move more slowly because they are not standing, lifeprotip: don't get up out of bed in the morning, spend all day slithering on the floor, be more effective or no less effective for less energy spent.
Rationale: The RAW is retarded in this case.

+Animal Handling Skill

1) Animal Handling is a separate skill under Trade skills, split from Survival. If you can take Survival during character creation, you can choose to take Animal Handling instead.

+Dark Theology Skill

1) Dark Theology is a separate skill under Scholar skills, split from Theology. If Theology covers 'the way things should be', Dark Theology covers things which are taboo or forbidden - diagnosing the Taint, being able to identify maho use, knowledge about oni etc. If you can take Theology in character creation and your backstory or clan function support it, then you can choose to take Dark Theology instead (i.e. a Kuni shugenja would probably have access to Dark Theology learning materials and mentors, but an Asahina artificer making art in some cushy Crane place would have no exposure under normal circumstances.)

+Taint House Rules

I am using a 1 to 100 scale for Taint, similar to Honor and Glory, where the Shadowlands Taint disadvantages happen when the character gets to 20, 40, 60, 80 and 100 points. This lets Taint happen to a character more than 5 times in their lives before they are Lost, lets the Taint grow over stime to consume the victim's mind and body slowly.

Tainted characters must make rolls to resist growth of the Taint using Fitness or Meditation.

Afflicted characters must make Resist Taint rolls as though they were Tainted.

Using evil items Afflicts, then Taints you. Using maho straight up taints you.

+Critical Strike Rules

Coming Soon, my issue here is that Dying is less bad than being drastically maimed, it is really hard to die as long as you have a half decent medic around but really super easy to get life changing injuries. I am sort of glad that you don't die by accident all the time, as I want to be able to make fights without worrying that Random Basic Bandit #348 will roll 3 10s on his damage roll and decapitate your samurai, because that is really uncinematic and also sort of disruptive - mainly I just want a smoother graph of Crit Severity-to-Badness rather than the weird up and down curve it makes at the moment.

+Equipment Adjustment

1) Traveling Clothes have a PR of 1.
RAW: Traveling Clothes give 2 PR.
Rationale: PR of 1 brings them in line with other clothes. Gives Concealed Armor and the other pseudo-armors a reason to exist.

2) Any time you can gain Ashigaru Armor during character creation, you can take Light Armor instead. It has the same stats.
RAW: There is no Light Armor.
Rationale: Ashigaru Armor is for scrubs, ronin and peasant scrubs. If you wear Ashigaru Armor, be ashamed. Light Armor is nice, smart battle armor with your clan mon, appropriate decoration and nice details on the edges.

+Cumbersome

If you are wearing Cumbersome armor, you suffer +1 TN with your weapon if you move before you make your Attack action.

RAW: Cumbersome armor just gives a +TN to movement actions. Cumbersome weapons give +1 TN to attacks if you move before attacking.
Rationale: I want there to be a penalty to Cumbersome armor. I want there to be a reason to wear Light Armor rather than whatever gives the most PR.
